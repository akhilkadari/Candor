# Candor ✨

**Recovery Journey Tracking with On-Device AI.**

Candor is a private, offline-first application designed to help individuals track their recovery journey. It provides tools to log daily check-ins, analyze patterns using on-device Large Language Models (LLMs), and maintain a secure history of progress.

## ✨ Core Features

* **Daily Check-In**: Track core metrics like craving intensity, mood, stress level, social connection, and self-efficacy.
* **On-Device Insights**: Use Gemma 4 E2B to analyze your log history directly on your device. Identify early signals, recurring patterns, and protective factors without your data ever leaving your phone.
* **Recovery History**: View and edit your past entries to maintain an accurate record of your journey.
* **100% Privacy**: All analysis happens on-device. No internet is required for core functionality, ensuring total privacy for your reflections and data.

## 🏁 Get Started

1. **Check OS Requirement**: Android 12 and up.
2. **Install & Explore**: Build the app locally following the development notes.

## 🛠️ Technology Highlights

* **Google AI Edge & LiteRT**: High-performance on-device ML runtime.
* **Gemma 4**: State-of-the-art open models for private text analysis.
